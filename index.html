Introduction

Play 2 Learn is an educational initiative designed to spark curiosity in young children through playful and engaging activities. Rather than using traditional teaching methods, our approach focuses on learning through play; helping children explore, ask questions, and enjoy the process of discovering new things.

At the heart of the program are trained youth volunteers, known as "Learning Buddies," who guide children through games that encourage curiosity, creativity, and joy. Learning Buddies are recent SEE graduates, high school students, undergraduate or graduate level students primarily from Makwanpaur district.

Play 2 Learn operates in schools through short sessions that include a variety of age-appropriate games and activities. These sessions are designed to fit into the school day without disrupting the regular routine. The games are carefully selected to keep children engaged while promoting skills such as observation, thinking, and problem-solving.

Our core belief is that curiosity is a natural gift in every child and by nurturing it early, we help build a strong foundation for lifelong learning. At its core, Play 2 Learn is about preserving and promoting one of the most powerful gifts we all share â€” curiosity.

Our Vision

Play 2 Learn is run by young people, for young people. Our vision is to provide a unique model of learning to young children which is interesting and easy to grasp. We inspire young volunteers to make a meaningful impact in their communities by engaging with one of the most important causes: education. We believe that with good ideas and a sincere heart, anyone can contribute to positive change.

Our focus is not to teach in the traditional sense, but to inspire a love of learning; encouraging children to explore, question, and grow with confidence. Additionally, through our program, we equip volunteers (Learning Buddies) with the skills to lead playful, curiosity-driven activities that bring joy to young children and spark their interest in learning.

We strive to inspire a sense of social responsibility in our volunteers.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create joyful, curiosity-driven learning experiences for young children by mobilizing passionate youth volunteers. We aim to nurture a love for learning that goes beyond classrooms and textbooks, making education fun, meaningful, and accessible.

Through simple games and thoughtful guidance, we want children to explore their natural curiosity, and help young volunteers grow into empathetic, responsible leaders who care deeply about their communities and the future of education in Nepal.
